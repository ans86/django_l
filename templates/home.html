Attack on Titan (進撃の巨人)

Humanity teeters on the brink of extinction, trapped behind massive walls to survive against terrifying, man-eating Titans. In this brutal world, young Eren Yeager witnesses the fall of his city and vows to destroy every last Titan. What begins as a fight for survival quickly spirals into a deep mystery about power, betrayal, and freedom itself.

Adapted from Hajime Isayama’s best-selling manga, Attack on Titan is a globally acclaimed anime epic filled with breathtaking action, rich storytelling, and emotional intensity.
